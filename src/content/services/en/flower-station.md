## Expert Testing Experience

Flower Station is a London-based florist offering 24/7 ordering and same-day delivery across the capital. Our 4-month testing period across 14 orders evaluated their service range from everyday bouquets to luxury arrangements, assessing delivery reliability, flower quality, and overall value for London customers.

Same-day delivery performance proved strong at 93% on-time completion within London, with most orders arriving within the stated 2-4 hour window. The 24/7 ordering system worked flawlessly, with orders placed late evening still dispatched for same-day delivery when within the cut-off window. Flower quality was consistently good, with arrangements lasting 6-9 days with proper care, though the 5-day freshness guarantee is conservative compared to competitors offering 7 or 10 days.

The product range impressed with its breadth. Budget-friendly options starting around £25 delivered solid quality, while luxury hat box arrangements and infinity rose collections justified their premium pricing through exceptional presentation. Gift add-ons including chocolates, candles, and teddy bears are well-curated and competitively priced. The corporate service demonstrated particular strength, with consistent quality across regular weekly orders for office environments.

Best suited for London residents and businesses who value same-day convenience, 24/7 availability, and the option to visit a physical store. The flower school courses add genuine value for those interested in floristry as a hobby. For customers outside London, the next-day UK delivery service is competent but faces stronger competition from nationally-focused services like Interflora and Bloom & Wild.
